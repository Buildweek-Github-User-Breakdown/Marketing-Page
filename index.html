With our app you can easily view other Github users, their top repositories, and their contributions at a glance. Sign up today and get full access to all of our app's features.

User Analysis

Get an accurate break-down of a Github user's activity. With our app, you can get data about a user's most common day of week and hour of day commit times, what languages are used in their repositories, and what most common language they typically work with.

Our app will also reveal the most popular repositories & repositories that the user has contributed to but not authored.
